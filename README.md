COSMOOD is a fun, beautifully designed webpage that tells you your "cosmic mood" for the day.
Think of it like a daily horoscope — but instead of star signs, it uses the current date and time to pick one of 8 poetic moods for you. Each mood has:

A dramatic name (like "The Restless Cartographer" or "Soft Rebellion")
A short poetic description of how you're feeling today
Your energy level, element, and cosmic hour
A personal mantra to carry through the day

You can also hit the "Recalibrate" button to shuffle to a different mood if the first one doesn't resonate.# cosmood
